Will food and drinks be available for purchase?

Yes! This year, we’re partnering with DeKalb Market Hall to bring in some of Brooklyn’s favorite food vendors. We’ll have kebabs, sandwiches, popcorn, cake pops, and so much more! To go along with these treats, beer and wine will be available for purchase, as well.

Can I bring my own food?

Of course — feel free to bring in your own food, but outside alcohol is still prohibited on the lawn. To that end, be advised that bag checks will be conducted at the entrance.

Why is there a bag check now?

Outside alcohol and glass bottles have always been prohibited in the Park. This year, we’re partnering with DeKalb Market Hall to bring food, beer, and wine vending to the Movies. With these new offerings, we want to remind you of this policy in order to ensure the safety and security of moviegoers.We hope you enjoy these new options!
